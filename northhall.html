Background

I lived in North Hall my sophomore year at SRU, and I've always admired its beautiful architecture. Upon wanting to learn Blender, I decided to model the building. I took several pictures of the building and used them as references to hopefully capture as much detail as possible. The whole process took about 6 months of on-and-off work, but I certainly learned a lot about 3D modeling, texturing, and rendering. Don't ask me how many vertexes this model has (It's way too many).
